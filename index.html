    About Me

    Hi, I'm Saarthak Gupta, a passionate Data Scientist with expertise in ML/NLP, skilled in building and deploying models. I have a unique blend of technical and analytical skills and am passionate about transforming data into actionable insights.

    I am a passionate and driven Mechanical Engineering student at IIT (BHU), Varanasi. I am particularly intrigued by the rapidly evolving field of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and have been diving deep into learning about generative AI and large language models (LLMs). With a continuous drive to expand my knowledge, I thrive on exploring innovative applications of AI in solving real-world problems. My interests lie at the intersection of data science and AI, and I am committed to leveraging these technologies to build intelligent, impactful solutions.

    I'm proficient in Python, SQL, and frameworks like Scikit-learn, pytorch, Langchain, HuggingFace, and TensorFlow. I love contributing to data science communities and sharing insights through my articles on Medium. My projects demonstrate a hands-on approach to solving real-world problems, and my work in data analysis and visualization has been recognized in competitions. I am always eager to learn, collaborate, and apply my skills to new challenges.

    I am currently seeking opportunities to work on challenging projects and collaborate with like-minded individuals. If you would like to discuss any such opportunities, please reach out to me.
